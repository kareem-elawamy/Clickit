ClickIt Project Update

Milestone 1 Completed (Performance & Security Overhaul)

I am pleased to report that we have successfully completed the first major milestone of the ClickIt system audit and optimization phase. Over the past period, we have identified and resolved 47 critical vulnerabilities and performance bottlenecks across your Web Admin Panel and Mobile Applications (Customer, Seller, and Delivery).

Our primary goal for this phase was to ensure the platform is highly scalable, incredibly fast, and secure enough to handle a massive influx of users without crashing or requiring expensive server upgrades.

Here is a high-level summary of what has been accomplished:

1. Mobile Apps Stabilization & Speed (Customer, Seller & Delivery)

  • We discovered and fixed several "Memory Leaks" that were causing the mobile apps to load slowly or crash for users with long histories (e.g., old delivery men or sellers with large catalogs).
  • Lightning-Fast Checkout: Completely rewrote the database queries for the Cart and Seller POS (Point of Sale). Operations that previously queried the database dozens of times per second now only query it 2-3 times, making checkouts instantaneous.
  • Instant Chat: Fixed a severe lag issue in the chat system across all three apps, allowing messages to load instantly regardless of how many active conversations the user has.
  • App Crash Prevention: Capped massive data loads (like full product lists, order histories, and refunds) ensuring the app uses minimal device memory.

2. Admin Panel & Web Optimization

The Admin dashboard is the brain of your operations. Previously, viewing reports or exporting data was forcing the server to load hundreds of thousands of records into its RAM, risking server crashes during peak hours.

  • Instant Analytics: Re-engineered how the dashboard calculates total customers, order statuses, and subscriber counts. Instead of counting records manually, we now utilize high-speed database algorithms.
  • Reliable Data Exports: Fixed a critical bug in the Order Export feature that caused the server to freeze when downloading large Excel/CSV files.
  • Optimized Ratings & Reviews: Streamlined how delivery man and seller ratings are calculated, making their profile pages load seamlessly.

3. Security Hardening

  • Upload Security: Completely replaced the outdated "blacklist" approach for file uploads with a strict "whitelist" validation, ensuring malicious files cannot be uploaded to your servers.
  • Endpoint Protection: Enforced strict authorization protocols on critical product-management pathways to prevent unauthorized modifications.
